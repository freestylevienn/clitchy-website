Corrugated Mabati

Traditional corrugated iron sheets with excellent strength and durability. Perfect for residential homes and housing projects.

Gauge 30: KES 495/meter
Gauge 28: KES 640/meter

  • ✓ Available in Glossy & Matte finishes

  • ✓ 5-year warranty

Box Profile Mabati

Modern rectangular profile with trapezoidal ridges. Offers superior strength and contemporary appearance for modern homes.

Gauge 30: KES 610/meter
Gauge 28: KES 730/meter
  • ✓ Modern sleek design for contemporary homes
  • ✓ Enhanced water drainage
  • ✓ Superior load bearing capacity
  • ✓ 10-year warranty
  • ✓ Perfect for residential roofing

Roman Tile Mabati

Elegant tile-like appearance with interlocking profile. Provides classic aesthetic with modern steel durability for premium homes.

Gauge 28: KES 680/meter
Gauge 26: KES 850/meter
  • ✓ Classic tile appearance for elegant homes
  • ✓ Interlocking design for extra protection
  • ✓ Longer lifespan for long-term value
  • ✓ 15-year warranty
  • ✓ Premium choice for residential properties

Mabati Gauge Comparison

Gauge Thickness (mm) Strength Level Best For Lifespan

28G 0.32 mm Standard Residential homes, standard buildings 15-20 years
26G 0.40 mm Strong Commercial buildings, heavy rainfall areas 25-30 years
